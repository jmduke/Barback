Jake Barnes

Ingredients

Applejack 4.5cl
Grenadine 2cl
Lemon juice 1.5cl
Pineapple juice 1.5cl
Angostura bitters One dash

Directions

Add all ingredients to shaker, fill completely with ice and shake vigorously.
