Moscow Mule

Ingredients

Vodka 4.5cl
Ginger beer 12cl
Lime juice 0.5cl

Directions

Combine the vodka and ginger beer. Add lime juice.

Tweet

Served in highball
% ABV
Garnish with Lime slice
