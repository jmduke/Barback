Brandy Crusta

Ingredients

Cognac 6cl
Orange curaçao One teaspoon
Angostura bitters One dash
Lemon juice Half teaspoon

Directions

Prepare glass by moistening the rim with lemon and dipping it in sugar, then carefully curling the lemon peel around the inside of the glass. Combine all ingredients in a shaker, shake and strain into glass and add 1 small cube of ice.
