Mint Julep

Ingredients

Bourbon 6cl
Water Two teaspoons
Powdered sugar One teaspoon
Mint sprigs Four

Directions

In the glass, gently muddle the mint, sugar and water. Fill the glass with cracked ice, add Bourbon and stir well until the glass is frost.
