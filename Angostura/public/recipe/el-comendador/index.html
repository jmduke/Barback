El Comendador

Ingredients

Dark rum 4.5cl
Mezcal 1.5cl
Pineapple juice 3cl
Lime juice 1.5cl
Kiwi slice

Directions

Combine ingredients in shaker tin. Muddle kiwi until crushed. Add a few pebbles of crushed ice and whip to froth pineapple juice. Dump into collins glass 1⁄2 filled with crushed ice. Top with crushed ice.
