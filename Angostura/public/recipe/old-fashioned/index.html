Old Fashioned

Ingredients

Whiskey Bourbon or rye whiskey x 4.5cl
Sugar cube
Angostura bitters

Directions

Place sugar cube in old fashioned glass and saturate with bitters, add a dash of plain water. Muddle until dissolve. Fill the glass with ice cubes and add whisky.
