French Martini

Ingredients

Vodka 4.5cl
Raspberry liqueur 1.5cl
Pineapple juice 1.5cl

Directions

Stir in mixing glass with ice cubes. Strain into chilled glass. Squeeze oil from lemon peel onto the drink.
