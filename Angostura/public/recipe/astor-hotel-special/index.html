Astor Hotel Special

Ingredients

Cognac 6cl
Lemon juice 2cl
Maraschino 2cl
Egg white One egg
Absinthe Rinse

Information

This delightful drink was originally concoted by the infamous Charles Baker for the Astor House Hotel. (You may have deduced this from the name.)

Directions

Add all ingredients to a shaker and shake without ice, add ice and shake vigorously, strain into glass. Top with chilled soda.
