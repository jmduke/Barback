Smith & Kearns

Ingredients

Coffee liqueur 6cl
Cream 6cl
Soda water 12cl

Directions

Pour the coffee liqueur and cream into a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well and strain into a highball filled with ice. Top with club soda.
