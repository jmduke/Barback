Painkiller

Ingredients

Dark rum Pusser's x 6cl
Pineapple juice 12cl
Orange juice 3cl
Coconut milk 3cl

Information

The official drink of Pusser’s Rum, who has an entertaining origin story for the drink. By default, the drink is weak – depending on your flavor profile (and ideal level of sobriety) bump up the amount of rum.

Directions

Fill a big glass or goblet with ice and add the ingredients. To mix, pour once or twice back and forth into another glass. Grate fresh nutmeg on top.
