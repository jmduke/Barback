Penicillin

Ingredients

Scotch 6cl
Laphroaig Or try another Islay x 1cl
Honey syrup 2cl
Lemon juice 2cl

Directions

Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice. Stir gently and strain into a rocks glass with a large ice cube.
