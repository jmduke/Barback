Tuxedo

Ingredients

Gin Old Tom Gin x 3cl
Vermouth Dry x 3cl
Orange bitters Three dashes
Maraschino Half barspoon
Absinthe Quarter barspoon

Directions

Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into glass.

Tweet

Served in martini
% ABV
Garnish with Cherry, lemon twist
