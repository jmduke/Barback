Irish Coffee

Ingredients

Cream 3cl
Whiskey Irish whiskey x 4cl
Coffee Hot x 9cl
Brown sugar One teaspoon

Directions

Warm the Irish whiskey over a burner. Pour into the glass (for hot drink) hot coffee, and add a teaspoon of sugar. Float Cream on top.
