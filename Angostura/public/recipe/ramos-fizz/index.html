Ramos Fizz

Ingredients

Gin 4.5cl
Lime juice 1.5cl
Lemon juice 1.5cl
Cream 6cl
Simple syrup 3cl
Vanilla extract Two drops
Orange flower water Three dashes
Egg white

Directions

Pour all ingredients (except soda) in a mixing glass, dry shake (no ice) for two minutes, add ice and hard shake for another minute. Strain into glass without ice, top with soda.
