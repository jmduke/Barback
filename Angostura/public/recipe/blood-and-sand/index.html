Blood and Sand

Ingredients

Scotch 2cl
Orange juice 2cl
Cherry brandy 2cl
Vermouth Italian x 2cl

Information

Named after the 1922 movie of the same name. First appeared in 1930, in the Savoy Cocktail Book.

Directions

Shake and strain into glass.
