Russian Spring Punch

Ingredients

Vodka 3cl
Lemon juice 3cl
Créme liqueur Créme de Cassis x 1.5cl
Simple syrup 1cl

Directions

Shake the ingredients and pour into glass. Top with Sparkling wine.
