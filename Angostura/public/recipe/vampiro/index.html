Vampiro

Ingredients

Orange juice 3cl
Tequila Silver x 5cl
Tomato juice 7cl
Lime juice 1cl
Worchestershire sauce Few dashes
Salt
Red chili peppers
Onion Half slice, finely chopped
Honey One teaspoon

Directions

Shake with ice cubes. Strain into glass, filled with ice.

Tweet
