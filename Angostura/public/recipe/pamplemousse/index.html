Pamplemousse

Ingredients

Gin London dry x 3cl
Grapefruit juice 3cl
Elderflower liqueur 1.5cl
Lemon juice 1.5cl

Directions

Pour all ingredients into rinsed shaker, shake on ice and double strain into glass.
