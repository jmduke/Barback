Boulevardier

Ingredients

Sweet vermouth 2cl
Campari 2cl
Bourbon 3cl

Information

From Harry McElhone”s 1927 Barflies and Cocktails, this drink is a darker cousin of the Negroni – having subtituted bourbon for gin.

Directions

Stir long and well with ice in a mixing glass and strain into a cocktail glass.
