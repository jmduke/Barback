Planter's Punch

Ingredients

Dark rum 4.5cl
Orange juice 3.5cl
Pineapple juice 3.5cl
Grenadine 1cl
Lemon juice 2cl
Simple syrup 1cl
Angostura bitters Three to four dashes

Information

To quote the magazine Fun, in 1878:

A wine-glass with lemon juice fill, Of sugar the same glass fill twice Then rub them together until The mixture looks smooth, soft, and nice.

Of rum then three wine glasses add, And four of cold water please take. A Drink then you’ll have that’s not badâ€” At least, so they say in Jamaica.

Directions

Pour all ingredients, except the bitters, into shaker filled with ice. Shake. Pour into large glass, filled with ice. Add Angostura bitters, “on top”.
