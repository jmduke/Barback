Angostura Sour

Ingredients

Angostura bitters 4.5cl
Simple syrup 3cl
Lime juice 2cl
Egg white One egg

Directions

Dry shake the egg white and the lime juice. Add the remaining ingredients, ice and shake hard. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.

Information

This cocktail was created by Kirk Estopinal and published in the book Beta Cocktails, a variation on the Angostura Fizz, which itself was published in Charles Baker’s The Gentlemen’s Companion in the 1930s, using half an ounce of bitters and additional rum.
