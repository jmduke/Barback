Kentucky River

Ingredients

Créme liqueur 0.5cl
Bourbon 6cl
Peach bitters Three dashes

Information

A mainstay cocktail from the 30’s. Essentially a twist on an old-fashioned; be sure to experiment with your amounts of each ingredient to find your personal balance of the competing flavors.

Directions

Build in glass with a large ice cube and then lightly stir to desired temperature.
