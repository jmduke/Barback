Diamondback

Ingredients

Rye 4.5cl
Applejack 3cl
Green Chartreuse 1.5cl

Information

Ostensibly named after the Diamondback Lounge in the Lord Baltimore Hotel. Has a bit more bite than the titular serpent, but with remarkably less poison.

Directions

Build in an old-fashioned glass over ice. Stir until chilled. Garnish with your own best wishes or deepest fears.
