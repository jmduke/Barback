Gin Fizz

Ingredients

Gin 4.5cl
Lemon juice 3cl
Simple syrup 1cl
Soda water 8cl

Directions

Shake all ingredients with ice cubes, except soda water. Pour into glass. Top with soda water.

Tweet

% ABV
Garnish with Lemon slice
