Brass Rail

Ingredients

White rum 6cl
Bénédictine 1.5cl
Lemon juice 1.5cl
Simple syrup Two dashes
Orange bitters One dash
Egg white One half

Directions

Dry shake, then shake on ice, and fine strain.
