Rye Smash

Ingredients

Rye 4.5cl
Water 3cl
Simple syrup 2cl
Lemon wedges Two
Mint leaves Two

Directions

In a shaker, muddle the mint, lemon, simple syrup and water. Add the rye and fill with ice. Shake and strain into a rocks-filled glass.

Information

Smashes are known for their refreshing quality – generally heavy on fruit and ice compared to other classes of cocktail, giving them a certain flexibility: you can dress one up for a summer afternoon or an autumn night equally well. Imbibe has a great piece on the history of the smash; for this Rye Smash, we recommend tinkering with the potency of the beverage to find your ideal level.
