Bellini

Ingredients

Prosecco 10cl
Peach puree 5cl

Directions

Pour peach puree into chilled glass and add sparkling wine. Stir gently. Variations: Puccini (fresh mandarin juice), Rossini (fresh strawberry puree), Tintoretto (fresh pomegranate juice)
