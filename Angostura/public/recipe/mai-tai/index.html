Mai-tai

Ingredients

Dark rum 2cl
White rum 4cl
Triple sec Orange Curaçao x 1.5cl
Orgeat 1.5cl
Lime juice 1cl

Directions

Shake and strain into glass. Serve with straw.

Tweet

Served in highball
% ABV
Garnish with Pineapple spear, mint leaves, lime wedge
