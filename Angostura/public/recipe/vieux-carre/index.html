Vieux Carré

Ingredients

Rye 3cl
Cognac 3cl
Vermouth Sweet x 3cl
Bénédictine 1cl
Peychaud’s bitters Two dashes
Angostura bitters Two dashes

Directions

Mix all ingredients in an old fashioned glass over ice; stir.
