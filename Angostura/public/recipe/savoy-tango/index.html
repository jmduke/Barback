Savoy Tango

Ingredients

Applejack Can substitute Calvados x 9cl
Sloe Gin 9cl

Information

This cocktail is beloved at it’s namesake: the Savoy Hotel in London, where it was invented.

Directions

Shake well and strain into cocktail glass.

Tweet
