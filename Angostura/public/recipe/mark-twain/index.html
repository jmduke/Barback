Mark Twain

Ingredients

Simple syrup 3cl
Lemon juice 2cl
Scotch 4.5cl
Angostura bitters Two dashes

Directions

Add all ingredients to a shaker and shake without ice, add ice and shake vigorously, strain into chilled cocktail glass.
