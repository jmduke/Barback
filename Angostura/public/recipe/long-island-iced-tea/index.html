Long Island Iced Tea

Ingredients

Tequila 1.5cl
Vodka 1.5cl
Gin 1.5cl
White rum 1.5cl
Triple sec 1.5cl
Lemon juice 2.5cl
Syrup Gomme x 3cl
Cola One dash

Directions

Add all ingredients into highball glass filled with ice. Stir gently. Serve with straw.
